3X3 Basketball

 

3X3 Basketball

Team Size : Minimum 5 Members in a Team

Each Game Play time is 25 Mins with 5 Mins Each Quarter which is divided into 4 Quarters.

Determining the Result:
1 Minute Break is given after each quarter and 2 Minutes in 3rd Quarter

30 Seconds TimeOut can be taken by each team in the Third Quarter and Fourth Quarters.

The game is played to 19 Points for Boys
                                    15 Points for Girls

Team will be considered winner either by reaching the Winning Points before the Timeline
(or)
Winner based on the Maximum Score at the end of the Match time

Scoring:
Beyond the arc is awarded 2 points
Inside the arc is awarded 1 point
Free Throws are awarded maximum 2 points (1 FT for inside the arc and 2 FT’s beyond the arc)

The teams will be divided into 2 categories
Tournament Format : Knock Out and Top 4 Teams in each category will go into Semis followed by Finals.

Age Categories
U-14 (2009) Boys - / Girls
U-16 (2007) Boys - / Girls
U-18 (2005) Boys

NOTE:
1. Wear your Team Jersey
2. Wear Basketball Shoes
3. Bring your own Water Bottles

Tournament Rules:

  • Please adhere to the rules of the game.
  • Referee decision is final.
  • Match Schedule will be shared 2 Days before the Tournament Day.
  • Please be available 10 Minutes before the time mentioned to you as per the Schedule.
  • Water is provided at the Venue
  • Bananas are provided at the Venue
  • Food is available at Food Counters (Should be ordered on your own)
