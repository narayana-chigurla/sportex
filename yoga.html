Art of Living Yoga Festival

 

Art of living serves society by strengthening the individual

Operating in 180 countries, The Art of Living is a non-profit, educational and humanitarian organization founded in 1981 by the world-renowned humanitarian and spiritual teacher - Gurudev Sri Sri Ravi Shankar. All our programs are guided by Gurudev''s philosophy: "Unless we have a stress-free mind and a violence-free society, we cannot achieve world peace." The Art of Living community is diverse and attracts people from all walks of life.

Yoga Fest

Immerse yourself in the transformative world of yoga at the Yoga Fest, presented by Art of Living and Sri Sri Yoga, taking place on the 25th and 26th of August. This two-day event offers something for everyone, with two distinct experiences Beyond Breath and Yogathon to choose from.

Benefits:
  • Learn postures, breathing techniques and meditation
  • Enhance flexibility and revitalise body and mind
  • Experience the stillness from experts
Day 1

Beyond Breath:

Immerse yourself in an incredible experience during the Day 1 event, Beyond Breath. This 2.5-hour session on 25th August from 5.30 pm to 8.00 pm, curated by Sri Sri Yoga, offers a sequence of powerful yoga asanas and a deep meditation experience. Here's what you can expect:

  • Powerful Yoga Asanas Sequence:
  • Calming Breathing Technique:
  • Deep Meditation Experience:
  • Keys to Mind Management:
  • Introduction to Sudarshan KriyaTM:
  • The Beyond Breath event is a holistic experience that integrates physical yoga, calming breathwork, deep meditation, and valuable insights into mind management. Join us for a transformative journey that will leave you feeling rejuvenated, centered, and equipped with powerful tools for navigating life's challenges.
Day 2

Yogathon Event:

Join us on Day 2 of Yoga Fest for Yogathon, a thrilling event where you'll perform Suryanamaskars under expert guidance. Taking place on 26th August from 7 am to 9.30 am, this 2.5-hour challenge offers three categories: Gold, Silver, and Bronze. Push your limits, showcase your strength, and be part of the transformative power of yoga. Choose your category and join us for an unforgettable experience at Yoga Fest's Yogathon.
